Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/1/2026

Intra-spect Reporting respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information Stored on Your Device

Intra-spect Reporting stores data locally on your device only. We do not operate a backend server and do not transmit your personal data to our own systems.

This local data may include:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Content you create or manage within the app
  • Images you choose to access or import into the app

b. Photos and Media Access

Intra-spect Reporting may request access to images or media stored on your device only when necessary for app functionality.

  • Images are accessed only at your direction
  • Images remain on your device
  • We do not upload, store, or share your images externally

You can revoke media access at any time through your device settings.

c. Subscription Information

  • The app offers paid subscriptions processed through Google Play Billing.
  • We do not collect or store payment details
  • Google Play may provide us with limited subscription status information (such as active or expired) to enable premium features
  • Payment data is handled according to Google’s privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information handled by the app is used solely to:

  • Provide and operate app features
  • Enable subscription-based functionality
  • Improve usability and performance
  • Maintain app stability and security

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information.

Because the app does not use a backend server:

  • Your data is not transmitted to us
  • Your data remains on your device
  • Your data is not shared with third parties, except as required for subscription processing through Google Play

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party service:

  • Google Play Services (for app distribution and subscription management)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app’s storage in device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Data Security

Because data remains on your device, its security depends on your device’s operating system and security settings.

We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where required by law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since data is stored locally, you control your data directly through the app and your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
